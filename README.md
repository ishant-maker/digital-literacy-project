## 📌 Project Overview
This portfolio is a part of the Digital Literacy course, aimed at developing essential digital skills required in academic and professional life. The project includes hands-on experience with presentation tools, building an online portfolio, using coding and collaboration platforms, understanding professional communication, and learning about cyber safety.

---

## 📚 Course Modules Summary

### 1. Hands-on Training on Presentation Software
In this module, I learned how to create presentations using digital tools like Canva. I designed an infographic covering digital literacy concepts, tools, safe internet practices, and email etiquette. This improved my creativity and presentation skills.

---

### 2. Building a Digital Portfolio
This module focused on creating a professional online presence. I created and updated my profiles on GitHub, LinkedIn, and Kaggle. These platforms will help me showcase my skills and projects in the future.

---

### 3. Programming Platforms and Collaborative Learning Platforms
In this module, I explored coding and collaboration tools. I solved a beginner-level problem on HackerRank and created a Digital Literacy quiz using Google Forms. This helped me understand practical learning and teamwork using digital platforms.

---

### 4. Email Etiquette and Basics of Social Media Etiquette
This module taught me how to write professional emails and maintain proper communication. I also learned the importance of responsible behaviour on social media and created a checklist of do’s and don’ts.

---

### 5. Awareness on Cyber Crime and Financial Fraud
In this module, I learned about cybercrimes like UPI fraud and how people get scammed online. I created a prevention checklist and understood the importance of staying alert while using digital payment systems.
